Welcome to Your Character Tips!

🛡 Combat & Survival Tips

  • AC 17 with Shield + Chain Shirt is already good, but remember: Shield of Faith can push it to 19 for clutch moments.
  • You’ve got Guiding Bolt — use it early in a fight to give your allies advantage on their next attack against that target.
  • Healing Word is your emergency heal — it’s a bonus action and works at range, so you can heal and still attack or cast another spell.
  • Bless is one of the best low‑level buffs in the game — cast it early in combat to boost your party’s attack rolls and saving throws.
  • You have Inflict Wounds — it’s risky (melee range), but it can hit like a truck if you need burst damage.

✨ Roleplay & Social Presence

  • You’ve got Persuasion +3 and Thaumaturgy — perfect for making speeches, calming crowds, or intimidating foes with divine theatrics.
  • Your Acolyte background gives you credibility with religious NPCs — temples will often give you shelter or aid.
  • Celestial is a rare language — use it for secret prayers, coded messages, or impressing divine beings.

📜 Spellcasting Reminders

  • You have a lot of prepared spells — don’t forget you can swap them after a long rest to adapt to the day’s challenges.
  • Mark which spells require Concentration (Bless, Shield of Faith, Protection from Evil and Good, Detect Magic, etc.) — you can only have one up at a time.
  • Your Magic Initiate (Cleric) feat gives you an extra cantrip and a free 1st‑level spell once per day — use that “free cast” strategically.

🧠 Tactical & Exploration Tips

  • Detect Magic and Detect Evil and Good are great for scouting dangerous areas before walking into trouble.
  • Purify Food and Drink and Create or Destroy Water can solve survival problems without spending resources.
  • Bane is underrated — lowering enemy attack rolls and saves can swing a fight in your favor.

🎯 Things to Remember in Play

  • Darkvision — you can see in dim light as if it’s bright, and in darkness as if it’s dim (but only in shades of gray).
  • Fey Ancestry — advantage on saves vs. charm, and immunity to magical sleep — don’t forget to remind your DM when it comes up.
  • Skill Versatility — you’ve got a broad skill set; even outside combat, you can contribute in investigations, diplomacy, and survival.

🔮 Growth Ideas for Later Levels

  • At level 2, you’ll get Channel Divinity — your domain choice will make this a big moment.
  • Consider upgrading to Scale Mail or Half Plate later for more AC.
  • Think about picking up War Caster or Resilient (Constitution) at higher levels to keep concentration spells running.
